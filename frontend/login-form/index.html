About the game

Welcome to the [Fostering Linux] Snake and Ladder Game!

Our game is not just about rolling dice and climbing ladders or getting bitten by snakes. It's an innovative approach to understanding our company's journey, its successes, and its challenges.

Why Play Our Game?

  • Learn About Our Business: Each step in the game will give you insights into our company's operations, strategies, and values.
  • Interactive Experience: Immerse yourself in a fun and interactive journey that combines entertainment with education.
  • Understand Profit and Loss: Experience firsthand the ups and downs of business, as snakes and ladders reflect our company's profits and losses.
  • Connect with Us: Get to know us better and understand what sets our company apart in the industry.

How to Play:

  1. Click the dice to make it rotate.
  2. If it lands on an achievement picture, you'll see a message and move forward.
  3. If it lands on a sad picture, you'll see a message and move backward.
  4. Once you reach a score of 100, click the dice again.
  5. Then, click on the "scores" button that appears.
  6. You'll be taken to a dashboard showing all the pictures you've seen and messages received.
  7. The dashboard has two sections: "Profit" for achievement messages and "Loss" for sad images, along with your scores.
  8. Clicking on "foxian" will take you to a fostering website.
